Siruvani Waterfalls

about tickets

It is situated at Siruvani Hills 37 km from Coimbatore and 6 km (3.7 mi) from Karunya University. Sadivayal Checkpost, where an entry fee of Rs. 50 is charged, and is at the gateway to the waterfalls. Private vehicles are not allowed beyond this point; however, vehicles arranged by the forest department can be used for the conveyance to the point where the trek is to be started. No extra charges for this service. An almost 1 km trek-walk through the forest is required to reach the exact place for bathing.
